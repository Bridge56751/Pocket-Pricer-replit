Pocket Pricer Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2026

⸻

1. Introduction

Pocket Pricer (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is a mobile application that helps users scan products and research resale prices on online marketplaces.

Pocket Pricer is owned and operated by Integrated Sales Solutions LLC.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Pocket Pricer mobile application and related services (the “Service”).

By using Pocket Pricer, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

⸻

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We may collect:
    •    Email address
    •    Account login credentials
    •    Securely hashed password (never stored in plain text)
    •    Subscription status
    •    Support communications

⸻

Automatically Collected Information
    •    Device type and operating system
    •    App usage data
    •    IP address (for security and fraud prevention)
    •    Login timestamps

⸻

Search History

Pocket Pricer stores a limited search history associated with your account, including approximately your last 15 searches.

This information is used solely to improve usability and allow you to revisit prior searches.

⸻

3. Camera Access

Pocket Pricer may request access to your device camera to:
    •    Scan products
    •    Capture item images for lookup and research

Camera access is optional and may be disabled at any time in device settings.

⸻

4. Photo Library Access

With your permission, Pocket Pricer may access your photo library to allow you to:
    •    Select existing product images
    •    Analyze selected images for pricing research

You may choose limited or full photo access.

Pocket Pricer accesses only photos you explicitly allow.

We do not:
    •    Access photos without permission
    •    Upload your entire photo library
    •    Sell or share personal images

⸻

5. Payments & Subscriptions

Payments are processed securely by Stripe.

Pocket Pricer does not store or process:
    •    Credit card numbers
    •    Bank account details

We store only subscription-related identifiers necessary to provide service access.

We use collected information to:
    •    Provide app functionality

    •    Enable premium features

    •    Comply with legal obligations

7. Data Security

We protect user data using:
