Pocket Pricer Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2026

⸻

1. Introduction

Pocket Pricer (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is a mobile application designed to help users scan products and research resale prices on online marketplaces.

Pocket Pricer is owned and operated by Integrated Sales Solutions LLC.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Pocket Pricer mobile application and related services (the “Service”).

By using Pocket Pricer, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

⸻

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

We may collect the following information:
    •    Email address
    •    Account login credentials
    •    Encrypted password (securely hashed — never stored in plain text)
    •    Subscription status
    •    Customer support communications

⸻

b. Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Service, we may automatically collect:
    •    Device type and operating system
    •    App usage activity
    •    IP address (used for security and fraud prevention)
    •    Login timestamps

⸻

c. Search History Data

Pocket Pricer stores a limited search history associated with your account, including:
    •    Your most recent product searches (approximately the last 15 searches)

This data is used solely to:
    •    Display search history to the user
    •    Improve app usability and performance

Search history is not shared publicly and is accessible only to the account owner.

⸻

3. Camera Access

Pocket Pricer may request access to your device camera in order to:
    •    Scan products
    •    Capture item images
    •    Assist with product identification and resale research

Camera access is optional and may be disabled at any time through your device settings.

Captured images are used only to provide app functionality.

⸻

4. Photo Library Access

With your permission, Pocket Pricer may request access to your device’s photo library in order to:
    •    Allow selection of existing product images
    •    Analyze user-selected photos for item lookup and pricing research

You may choose to grant:
    •    Limited photo access (selected images only), or
    •    Full photo library access

Pocket Pricer accesses only the images you explicitly allow.

We do not:
    •    Access photos without permission
    •    Upload your entire photo library
    •    Use photos for advertising or tracking
    •    Sell or share personal images

You may revoke photo access at any time through your device’s privacy settings.

⸻

5. Payments and Subscriptions

Pocket Pricer offers optional paid subscription features.

All payments are processed securely by Stripe.

Pocket Pricer does not collect or store:
    •    Credit card numbers
    •    Bank account details
    •    Payment authentication information

We store only:
    •    Stripe customer ID
    •    Stripe subscription ID
    •    Subscription status

Payment information is governed by Stripe’s privacy policy.

⸻

6. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
    •    Create and manage user accounts
    •    Authenticate logins
    •    Provide scanning and pricing functionality
    •    Manage subscriptions and billing
    •    Enable premium features
    •    Improve app performance and reliability
    •    Prevent fraud and abuse
    •    Comply with legal obligations

⸻

7. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard security practices, including:
    •    Password hashing using bcrypt
    •    Encrypted database connections
    •    Secure server infrastructure
    •    Environment-based secret storage
    •    HTTPS encryption

User data is stored on protected servers with restricted access.

⸻

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

Information may be shared only with:
    •    Stripe (payment processing)
    •    Infrastructure and hosting providers required to operate the Service
    •    Law enforcement or regulators when legally required

⸻

9. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to operate the Service or comply with legal requirements.

Users may request account deletion at any time.

⸻

10. Your Rights

You may:
    •    Access your personal information
    •    Update or correct your data
    •    Cancel subscriptions
    •    Request deletion of your account

Requests can be made through the app or by contacting support.

⸻

11. Children’s Privacy

Pocket Pricer is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

⸻

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

Updates will be posted within the app or on our website with a revised effective date.
