Privacy Policy

Pocket Pricer
Last updated: January 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Pocket Pricer ("we," "our," or "us") is a mobile application designed to help users scan products and research resale prices on online marketplaces.

Pocket Pricer is owned and operated by Integrated Sales Solutions LLC.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Pocket Pricer mobile application and related services (the "Service").

By using Pocket Pricer, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Email address
  • Account login credentials
  • Encrypted password (securely hashed â€” never stored in plain text)

b. Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Service, we may automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system

c. Search History Data

Pocket Pricer stores a limited search history associated with your account, including:

Search history is not shared publicly and is accessible only to the account owner.

3. Camera Access

Pocket Pricer may request access to your device camera in order to:

  • Scan products
  • Capture item images
  • Assist with product identification and resale research

Camera access is optional and may be disabled at any time through your device settings.

Captured images are used only to provide app functionality.

4. Photo Library Access

With your permission, Pocket Pricer may request access to your device's photo library in order to:

  • Allow selection of existing product images
  • Analyze user-selected photos for item lookup and pricing research

You may choose to grant:

  • Limited photo access (selected images only), or
  • Full photo library access

Pocket Pricer accesses only the images you explicitly allow.

We do not:

  • Access photos without permission
  • Upload your entire photo library
  • Use photos for advertising or tracking
  • Sell or share personal images

You may revoke photo access at any time through your device's privacy settings.

5. Payments and Subscriptions

Pocket Pricer offers optional paid subscription features.

All payments are processed securely by Stripe.

Pocket Pricer does not collect or store:

  • Credit card numbers
  • Bank account details
  • Payment authentication information

We store only:

  • Stripe customer ID
  • Stripe subscription ID
  • Subscription status

Payment information is governed by Stripe's privacy policy.
